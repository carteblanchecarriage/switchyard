Gaming Specs Explained

Polling Rate: How often the keyboard reports to your PC. 1000Hz = 1ms. Higher is better, but diminishing returns after 1000Hz.

N-Key Rollover (NKRO): Press any number of keys simultaneously, all register. Essential for games with complex inputs.

Actuation Force: How hard you press before the key registers. Lighter (45g) = faster for gaming. Heavier = fewer accidental presses.

Debounce Time: Delay to prevent "chattering" (one press registering twice). Optical switches have near-zero debounce.
