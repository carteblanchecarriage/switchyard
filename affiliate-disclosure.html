Our Affiliate Relationships

Switchyard participates in affiliate marketing programs with various keyboard vendors and retailers. This means when you click on certain links and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you.

How It Works

  1. You click a link on Switchyard to a vendor's product page
  2. The link contains a tracking code that identifies Switchyard as the referrer
  3. If you make a purchase, the vendor pays us a small percentage of the sale
  4. You pay the same price as if you visited the vendor directly

Why We Use Affiliates

Affiliate revenue is how we fund Switchyard operations:

  • Development time for new features
