# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 19, 2026

## Overview

Keyboard Tracker aggregates publicly available data about mechanical keyboard group buys and interest checks. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data**: Page views, search queries, API calls (anonymized)
- **Analytics**: Aggregate visitor statistics (no personal identification)

### User-Provided (Optional - Future Feature)
- Email addresses (only if you sign up for alerts - not yet implemented)
- Saved tracked items (associated with your account)

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Personal identifiers (name, address, phone)
- ❌ Payment information (handled by Stripe/PayPal if implemented)
- ❌ Reddit/Geekhack login credentials
- ❌ Private messages or non-public forum data

## Data Sources

We only aggregate **publicly available** data from:
- Reddit (r/MechanicalKeyboards, r/mechmarket)
- Geekhack.org public forums
- Vendor public product listings

All scraped data is already publicly visible to anyone visiting these sites.

## How We Use Data

1. **Display**: Show group buys, interest checks, and vendor listings
2. **Search**: Enable filtering and searching of aggregated data
3. **Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve the service
4. **Revenue Tracking**: Track affiliate link clicks (anonymous)

## Affiliate Links

Some links are affiliate links. We earn commission when you purchase through these links at **no extra cost to you**. This is clearly disclosed on all pages containing affiliate links.

## Third-Party Services

- **Payments**: Stripe/PayPal for subscriptions (not yet implemented)
- **Hosting**: Vercel/Netlify (when deployed)
