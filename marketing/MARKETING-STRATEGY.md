## Risk Mitigation

**What if Reddit post gets downvoted?**
- Delete and repost with modified content
- Ask in community thread "What do you think of this?" instead of direct promo
- Focus on Discord engagement instead

**What if vendors complain about scraping?**
- Have robots.txt explanation ready
- Offer to optimize their SEO/direct traffic
- Offer data partnership (we send them traffic, they give us API access)
- Worst case: Remove vendor and move on

**What if affiliate links are flagged?**
- Disclose prominently
- Lead with value, not sales
- Make tool useful without clicking links

**What if we run out of growth ideas?**
- Survey existing users
- Analyze competitor strategies
- Expand to adjacent niches (gaming peripherals, office tech)
