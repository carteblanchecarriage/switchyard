The Three Types of Switches

üîµ Linear

No bump, no click. Smooth from top to bottom. Great for gaming and fast typing. Quietest option.

  • Smooth consistent feel
  • Fast double-tapping
  • Quiet operation

Best for: Gaming, open offices

üü¢ Tactile

You feel the actuation. A little "bump" tells you the key registered. No click sound.

  • Tactile feedback
  • Quieter than clicky
  • Good for accuracy

Best for: Typing, all-around use

üî¥ Clicky

Bump + Click. Classic mechanical keyboard sound. Satisfying but loud. Not for shared spaces.

  • Audible satisfaction
  • Tactile + sound
  • Retro feel

Best for: Home offices, enthusiasts
