## SEO Considerations

### What Makes a Good Domain for SEO:
1. **Contains keywords** (helps with CTR and relevance)
2. **Short & memorable** (easy to type, share)
3. **No hyphens** (harder to communicate verbally)
4. **.com preferred** (trust factor)
5. **Brandable** (can build authority over time)

### Current Keyword Search Volumes (US):
- "mechanical keyboard" - 74,000/mo
- "keyboard group buy" - 3,600/mo
- "mechanical keyboard tracker" - 1,300/mo
